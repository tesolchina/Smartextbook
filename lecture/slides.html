The Challenge
Textbooks Are Static.
Learners Are Not.
  • Traditional educational materials are fixed — same content, same sequence, same depth for every student
  • Modern students need personalised pacing, immediate feedback, and learning pathways matched to their prior knowledge
  • The result: disengagement, knowledge gaps, and a "one-size-fits-none" experience
  • Educators know this — but have no accessible tools to fix it
📚
Most textbooks in use today were written before the smartphone — let alone generative AI
The Core Paradox

AI can now generate bespoke learning experiences — yet 95% of classroom content remains static PDFs and printed books.

AI Works — But Isn't Accessible

The Gap We're Closing
Technical Barriers
Are Gatekeeping AI Education
  • Existing AI-augmented tools require API integrations, prompt engineering, hosting infrastructure — all beyond typical educators
  • Ed-tech platforms offer generic AI assistants — not ones that understand your specific course material
  • The "last mile" problem: great AI exists, but educators cannot drive it
  • Our question: can we make this as easy as writing a document?
Our Research Question

Can a non-technical educator — without writing a single line of code — produce an AI tutor that deeply understands their subject content and is ready for students within minutes?

The Barrier Breakdown

Technical setup · Prompt design · Content grounding · Deployment · Maintenance

Our Approach
"Vibe Coding":
Natural Language as the Interface
Coined by Andrej Karpathy (2025), "vibe coding" describes the practice of directing AI systems using plain natural language — describing what you want rather than coding how to build it.
In Our Platform

An educator types: "My students struggle with passive voice. Be strict but encouraging. Focus on academic writing style."

The platform translates this into a fully configured content-aware AI tutor — no code, no settings panels.

Traditional Approach
  • Write system prompts
  • Configure API endpoints
  • Ground in content manually
  • Deploy & maintain
Vibe
Educator types one sentence.
Platform handles everything else — grounding, tone, domain focus, deployment.
